The Flash

Barry Allen uses his super speed to go back in time to change the events of the past. However, his actions cause unintended consequences that alter the future, trapping him in a reality where General Zod has returned, and there are no superheroes to save the day. To fix his world and return to the present, Barry enlists the help of a retired Batman, another version of himself, and an imprisoned Kryptonian.
