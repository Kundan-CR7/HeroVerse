Wonder Woman 1984

Diana Prince lives quietly among mortals in the vibrant, sleek 1980s—an era of excess driven by the pursuit of having it all. Though she's come into her full powers, she maintains a low profile, curating ancient artifacts and performing heroic acts incognito. But now, the world is in grave danger from an artifact granting wishes at a cost. To stop the chaos, Wonder Woman must face two formidable foes: Maxwell Lord and The Cheetah.
