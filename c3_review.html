Batman: Riddled by the Court of Owls

When the enigmatic Riddler's schemes collide with the shadowy agenda of the Court of Owls, Gotham City's darkest secrets come to light. As Batman races to untangle their sinister plans, he must confront his deepest fears and face the possibility that even his trusted allies might be pawns in a greater game.
