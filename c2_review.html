X-Men: Raid on Graymalkin

The X-Men, led by Professor Charles Xavier, embark on a daring raid on Graymalkin, the secret base of the mutant Cable. As they confront Cable's security systems and defenses, secrets about his past and the connection between Graymalkin and the X-Men's own future unravel. What they discover could reshape their understanding of the mutant legacy forever.
