Batgirl: Sins of the Mother

Batgirl's mother, the notorious criminal and assassin, returns to Gotham, forcing Barbara Gordon to confront her painful past. As the city falls into chaos under the influence of her mother's machinations, Batgirl must decide whether to honor her commitment to justice or give in to her family's dark legacy.
